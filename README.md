# Kyrylo P | E-Commerce Analyst (SQL · Python · Data Cleaning · BI)

---

## About Me

I help small-to-medium e-commerce businesses to make sense of messy, incomplete, and scattered data so they can understand what is actually happening in their sales, customers, and marketing performance.

My focus is on solving practical business questions such as:
- Which products actually drive revenue?
- Which customers are likely to return or churn?
- Where is marketing spend efficient or wasted?
- How can pricing and discounts improve profitability?

I work across the full analytics stack — from raw data extraction to final dashboards and reporting.

---

## What I Can Help With

I support e-commerce businesses by translating operational and marketing data into clear performance metrics across:

- Customer behavior & segmentation (RFM, cohort analysis, customer lifecycle patterns)  
- Product performance & pricing analysis (demand, value distribution, pricing tiers)  
- Revenue, retention, and growth tracking (LTV, cohorts, repeat behavior)  
- Marketing and funnel performance analysis (conversion patterns, product engagement, traffic-to-sales flow)  
- Data cleaning, transformation, and modeling (SQL/Python pipelines, dataset structuring)  
- KPI dashboards for decision-making and reporting

I have a strong understanding of core e-commerce and marketing KPIs (such as AOV, conversion rate, retention rate, LTV, ROAS, and churn), which allows me to align technical analysis with actual business decision-making rather than purely technical outputs.
